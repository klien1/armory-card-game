Objective

  • Team up with allies to defeat the boss.
  • Win by reducing the boss's health to zero!
  • You lose if all players have zero health.

How to play

  • At the beginning of the game you can choose a class to play.
  • Once two players are ready, the game will start.
  • When the game starts you can perform a number of actions during your turn.
    • Move your character one space (diagonal spaces count as two spaces).
    • Play cards from your hand.
    • Attack.
  • At the end of your turn, you draw up to fives cards.
  • After all players have taken a turn, the boss takes two actions.
  • Once the boss is done with its actions, it will be the players' turn.
